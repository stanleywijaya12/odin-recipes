Cheesy Ramen's Recipe

Description

Cheesy Ramen, good for cheesy person

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups water
  • 1 (3 ounce) package any flavor ramen noodles
  • 1 slice American Cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan. Add ramen noodles and cook until tender
  3. Pour out water, then stir in seasoning packet and cheese until well blended
